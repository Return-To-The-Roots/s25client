# Buildings: Tools & goods production

These buildings create **tools**, **weapons**, and **construction materials** used by settlers and soldiers.

## Toolmaker

### What it does
Produces **tools** required by new workers.

### Key points
- Without tools, settlers cannot become workers.
- Tools include axes, bows, scythes, hammers, etc.
- One toolmaker is usually enough for most of the game.

## Armory

### What it does
Produces **weapons and shields**.

### Inputs / outputs
- Inputs: **iron**, **coal**
- Outputs: **swords**, **shields**

### Key points
- Weapons are required to create soldiers.
- Place near storehouses to ensure steady supply.

## Weaponsmith balance

- Overproducing weapons wastes iron and coal.
- Underproducing weapons slows military growth.
- Adjust production based on expansion and war needs.
